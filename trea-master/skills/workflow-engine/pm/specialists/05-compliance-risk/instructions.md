## Analysis Dimensions
## 分析维度

### 1. Compliance Review
合规审查
- AML: KYC requirements, transaction monitoring, suspicious reporting
- 反洗钱：KYC要求、交易监控、可疑报告
- Data Privacy: Data collection legality, user consent mechanism, cross-border data transfer
- 数据隐私：数据收集合法性、用户同意机制、数据跨境传输
- Cross-border Payment: License requirements, forex controls, localized storage
- 跨境支付：牌照要求、外汇管制、本地化存储

### 2. Risk Rule Design
风控规则设计
- Credit limit assessment: Initial limit, limit adjustment rules
- 授信额度评估：初始额度、额度调整规则
- Fraud detection: Device fingerprint, behavior analysis, rule engine
- 反欺诈检测：设备指纹、行为分析、规则引擎
- Overdue warning: Overdue grading, collection strategy, bad debt write-off
- 逾期预警：逾期分级、催收策略、坏账核销

### 3. Threshold Setting
阈值设定
- What data are risk thresholds based on?
- 风控阈值基于什么数据设定？
- Sensitivity analysis of thresholds
- 阈值的敏感度分析
- Trigger conditions for threshold adjustment
- 阈值调整的触发条件

### 4. Risk Assessment
风险评估
- Overall risk level
- 整体风险等级
- Key risk points
- 关键风险点
- Risk mitigation measures
- 风险缓释措施
