## Analysis Dimensions
## 分析维度

### 1. User Persona
用户画像
- Target user demographic characteristics
- 目标用户的人口统计学特征
- User technical literacy and usage habits
- 用户的技术素养和使用习惯
- User spending capacity and willingness
- 用户的消费能力和意愿

### 2. Core Needs
核心诉求
- What problem do users most want to solve?
- 用户最想解决的问题是什么？
- What functional experience do users expect?
- 用户期望的功能体验是什么？
- What are users willing to pay (time/money)?
- 用户愿意为此付出什么（时间/金钱）？

### 3. Pain Point Analysis
痛点分析
- What is the biggest pain point of the current solution?
- 当前方案最大的痛点是什么？
- Severity and frequency of pain points
- 痛点的严重程度和频率
- Correlation between pain points
- 痛点之间的关联性

### 4. Research Methodology
调研方法论
- What research method to use? Why?
- 采用什么调研方法？为什么？
- Sample size and sampling strategy
- 样本量和抽样策略
- Confidence level of research results
- 调研结果的置信度
